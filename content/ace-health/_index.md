      text: |
        The **ACE-Health** group at Nanyang Technological University investigates the intersection of climate extremes and human health. Our work spans three core pillars:

        - **AI-driven Climate Extremes Attribution** — Using machine learning and causal inference to attribute extreme heat events and understand their drivers under climate change.
        - **Multiscale Health Projection** — Projecting future heat-related health burdens — including psychiatric disorders and cardiovascular diseases — across populations and regions under climate change scenarios.
        - **Climate-Health Burden Mitigation** — Developing multi-phase early warning systems and low-cost intervention strategies to protect vulnerable populations and support public health decision-making.

        We combine epidemiology, atmospheric science, and AI to generate actionable insights for climate adaptation and resilience.
